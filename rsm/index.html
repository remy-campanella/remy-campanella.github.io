RSM Project

Author’s OOC in reality warning!!!
作者现实人设崩坏警告！！！

RSM Project, fully named Reminiscences de Snowflake Mapleleaf Project, is an fiction-oriented art creation project of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic(MLP: FiM, yes, the very one). It mainly consists of a novel(Reminiscences de Snowflake Mapleleaf), an art setting collection(hopefully), and a music collection(probably), centered around author’s MLP OC, Snowflake Mapleleaf.

You cannot find the novel here. Chapters are normally arranged in the blog system there.
